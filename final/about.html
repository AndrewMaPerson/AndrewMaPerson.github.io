Our story

Once upon a time, in the bustling city of Aberdeen, two star-crossed lovers embarked on a journey that would transform their lives and captivate the hearts of food enthusiasts for years to come. Li Mei and Chen Wei, hailing from a small village in China, had ventured to America in search of a better future in 2012. However, fate dealt them a difficult hand, as they struggled to find employment in their newfound land. Their dreams of a prosperous life seemed to be slipping away like fleeting shadows.

Amidst the relentless job hunt, Li Mei, a radiant soul with a passion for cooking, found solace in her kitchen. Her culinary prowess and profound love for Chinese cuisine ignited a spark in her heart. Chen Wei, a diligent and resourceful man, recognized his beloved's talent and unwavering dedication. He became the pillar of support she needed during those challenging times, encouraging her to pursue her dreams. Together, they concocted a plan to bring the flavors of their homeland to the vibrant streets of Aberdeen.

In 2016, Li Mei and Chen Wei opened the doors to the enchanting Aberdeen Cafe. Nestled in the heart of the city, the café exuded an ambiance of romance and elegance. Drawing inspiration from their arduous journey, the couple infused the restaurant with their own love story, creating a haven where passion and culinary excellence intertwined. Embracing the Toyota philosophy they had learned during Chen Wei's days in factory administration, the couple instilled a meticulous attention to detail and unwavering commitment to quality in every aspect of the café's operations.

Aberdeen Cafe soon became synonymous with excellence, renowned for its boutique offerings and uncompromising commitment to taste. Li Mei's culinary creations were a symphony of flavors, tantalizing the palates of patrons and transporting them to the vibrant streets of China. The menu boasted an array of delicacies, but three dishes stood out as the crowned jewels of the café's culinary repertoire: the fiery Spicy Sichuan Beef, the vibrant Stir-fried Chinese Broccoli, and the exquisite Shao Mai. Each bite was a testament to Li Mei's passion and skill, leaving guests enchanted by the harmonious blend of aromas and the rich tapestry of flavors.

As time passed, Aberdeen Cafe became more than just a restaurant; it became a testament to the power of love, resilience, and the pursuit of dreams. Li Mei and Chen Wei's love story intertwined with the scents and flavors of their dishes, casting a spell on all who entered. Their tale served as a reminder that even amidst adversity, love and passion have the power to create something truly extraordinary. And so, Aberdeen Cafe continued to flourish, its doors forever open, inviting lovers, dreamers, and food enthusiasts to experience a taste of romance and indulge in the magic of Li Mei and Chen Wei's culinary masterpiece.
